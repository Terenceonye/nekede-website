REGISTRAR

Office of the Registrar

Mrs. Anuna E. C.

The Registry is the department concerned with the day-to-day administration of the Polytechnic. It derives its functions and responsibilities from that of the Registrar as defined in the Polytechnic Bye-Law. The Registry is headed by the Registrar, who is the Chief Administrative Officer of the Polytechnic.

The Registrar as the Head of the Polytechnic Administration, under the supervision of the Rector, provides services to enable the Polytechnic perform its tripartite function of teaching, research and community service by guiding and facilitating the work of the various committees constituted for the proper running of the Polytechnic. The Registrar facilitates the functions of the organs of governance and their committees in his capacity as Secretary to the statutory bodies i.e. The Governing Council, Congregation and Convocation.

Every activity of the Polytechnic whether academic, administrative, financial or physical development is subsumed under one or other of these organs of governance and thus, the Registry is the most vital department in the management and administration of the Polytechnic.

The Office of the Registrar also provides customer services regarding admission/enrollment, conducting registration and certifying graduation requirements through its various units.
