The Office of the Deputy Rector (Administration) manages the institution’s administrative functions. This office oversees staff recruitment, development, and welfare, as well as the maintenance of facilities and resources.

The Deputy Rector (Administration) ensures smooth day-to-day operations, handles non-academic policies, and coordinates the activities of administrative units to support the institution’s overall goal
