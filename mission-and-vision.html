Mission, Vision, and Objectives

Mission

Established to address the need for skilled manpower in science, engineering, and technology, Federal Polytechnic, Nekede is committed to producing graduates who are not only self-employable but also job creators. Our mission is to: Nurture and sustain the best practices in teaching, learning, research, and technological innovations to produce job creators for the global economy.

Vision

Federal Polytechnic, Nekede aims to be a leader in demand-driven technology and innovation, as captured in our Vision Statement: To Be Pacesetters In Demand-Driven Technology And Innovations. This vision aligns with our Motto: Knowledge and Skill for Service, highlighting our commitment to advancing scientific and technological knowledge for the benefit of humanity. Our popular slogan, “Federal Polytechnic, Nekede leads and others follow,” reflects this dedication.

Objectives

  • Providing training, research, and development in applied arts, sciences, technology, management, and commerce.
  • Offering full-time and part-time courses leading to diplomas, certificates, and other qualifications.
  • Preparing students for examinations set by the Institution or other examining bodies.
  • Developing techniques to meet the needs of Imo State and Nigeria.
  • Organizing conferences, seminars, and public lectures to disseminate knowledge.
