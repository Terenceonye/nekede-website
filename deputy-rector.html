Office of the Deputy Rector (Academics)

Dr Chris Onyemenonu

The Office of the Deputy Rector (Academics) typically oversees academic affairs and ensures the smooth operation of academic programs.This office is responsible for implementing policies related to teaching, research, curriculum development, and academic staff matters.

The Deputy Rector (Academics) coordinates academic planning, quality assurance, and the overall academic calendar, working closely with heads of departments, faculty members, and students to maintain academic standards and promote innovation in teaching and learning.
